FAQ

Everything you need to know

What platforms does Edge Vault sync with?

+

Edge Vault auto-syncs with any MT4 or MT5 account via a lightweight expert advisor, plus HTML file import from TradingView and cTrader, and manual entry for any other market or platform, including crypto, stocks, futures, and options. Your data syncs in real time across devices.

Does Edge Vault place trades for me?

+

No. Edge Vault is a trading journal, not an auto-trader. It tracks, analyzes, and helps you review your trades. It never executes or places orders on your behalf.

What happens when I hit a Daily Limit?

+

Once you cross your Daily Limit, Edge Vault flags the session with an instant notification and locks new trade entries, giving you a hard stop to protect your account from revenge trading or overtrading.

Is my trading data secure?

+

Yes. Account connections run through secure, authenticated API sessions, and your journal data is stored with account-level access rules in a secure cloud database, so no one else can see your trades.

Can I import old TradingView or cTrader history?

+

Yes. Export your trade history as an HTML file from TradingView or cTrader and import it directly. Edge Vault parses and deduplicates it automatically. You can also manually log past trades and attach chart screenshots.

Do I need to install anything?

+

No. Edge Vault runs entirely in your browser as a web app, so there's nothing to download or install.
